## 1. Detailed content brief

## Brand context

**The Wire Brand** is a handcrafted boutique wine farm in Paarl. The site should feel warm, premium, personal, and grounded in land, craft, and hospitality. The tone should be approachable and passionate, not formal or overly luxurious.

## Content goals

1. Position the winery as a distinctive handcrafted wine brand with a believable origin story.
2. Sell physical products and bookable experiences through the shop.
3. Use storytelling to support premium pricing and emotional attachment.
4. Introduce a memorable wine box subscription as the flagship ecommerce offer.
5. Build a content structure that works for a WordPress shop plus editorial pages and event listings.

## Core story pillars

### Origin story

Focus on:

* the heat and character of Paarl
* the valley landscape and vineyard conditions
* a small-batch, hands-on approach
* a founding story tied to craft, patience, and a love of wine
* the meaning of “The Wire Brand” as a mark of care, identity, and old-world craft

Suggested direction:
The brand began as a small cellar experiment shaped by hot valley days, long harvest evenings, and a belief that great wine is made by attention, not scale. The name “The Wire Brand” evokes both vineyard structure and the maker’s mark — something bound, shaped, and signed by hand.

### History

Create a concise but evocative timeline:

* early vineyard roots or family farming heritage
* experimental first vintages
* first release
* first tasting room weekends
* expansion into wine boxes and events
* recent recognition or regional momentum

For demo purposes, keep it realistic and boutique rather than making the brand look industrial or overly established.

### Awards and recognition

Use these as premium trust signals, but keep them modest and plausible for a boutique producer.

Suggested categories:

* small-batch red of the year shortlist
* local Paarl tasting circle recognition
* regional harvest selection mention
* design and label recognition
* cellar door hospitality mention

For demo content, present these as **placeholder awards** unless you plan to replace them with real achievements.

## 2. 3D WebGL subscription box concept

This is the perfect signature visual moment.

## Creative direction

Create a premium interactive 3D box for the wine subscription product. It should feel tactile, crafted, slightly theatrical, and gift-worthy.

## Visual behaviour

* A textured branded wine box sits in 3D space.
* Slow ambient rotation or parallax response on pointer movement.
* The lid lifts or slides slightly on hover or scroll cue.
* Inside the box, bottles, tissue wrap, tasting cards, or vineyard notes are partially visible.
* Light catches embossed logo or printed label details.
* Optional soft dust, grain, or paper fleck atmosphere.
* Optional vineyard contour lines or hand-drawn wire graphics appear around it as layered 2D overlays.

## Styling details

* corrugated premium cardboard texture
* branded stamp or ink print mark
* hand-drawn line details on the packaging
* muted wine-farm palette
* warm paper and ink tones
* premium but human, not glossy luxury tech

## UX rules

* all product information must still exist as standard HTML content
* 3D is supportive, not essential
* provide static fallback for mobile or low-power devices
* respect reduced motion preferences
