## 9. Motion direction

Use motion as craft and atmosphere, not gimmick.

## Motion style

* slow drift
* parallax layers
* sketch-line reveal
* bottle label shimmer
* grain movement
* light paper flutter
* contour-line animations
* liquid-inspired easing
* off-axis hover motion
* subtle 3D tilt on cards or bottles

## WebGL / 2D / 3D guidance

Use advanced graphics in a **controlled** way:

### Good uses

* hero background depth
* floating vineyard topology lines
* animated sun haze
* abstract grape/vine particle field
* premium bottle showcase
* atmospheric transitions between sections

### Avoid

* constant motion everywhere
* flashy effects that undermine readability
* heavy graphics inside every card
* unusable scroll performance on mobile

## Accessibility and performance rules

* respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
* always provide non-WebGL fallbacks
* do not make critical content dependent on canvas
* keep text on HTML layers
* use graphics to support atmosphere, not core comprehension
