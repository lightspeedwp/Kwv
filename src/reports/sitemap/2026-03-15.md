### 4. Route Organization Issues

**Problem:** Sitemap doesn't distinguish between:
- **Primary routes** (canonical URLs)
- **Alternative paths** (aliases/redirects)
- **Dynamic routes** (parameterized)
- **Utility routes** (system/meta pages)

- Primary: `/cart`
- Alternative: `/shop/cart` (not documented)
