## Key Changes from Current Structure

### ✅ Benefits of New Navigation:

1. **Simplified** - One header instead of 5 different headers
2. **Intuitive** - Clear categories (About, Shop, Visit, Events)
3. **Family-Friendly** - Warm, welcoming language
4. **Experience-First** - "Visit" prominently featured
5. **Unified Brand** - Consistent across entire site
6. **Mobile-Optimized** - Clean accordion structure

### ❌ Removed Complexity:

- ❌ Multiple brand portfolios (Cathedral Cellar, Laborie, etc.)
- ❌ Corporate language ("KWV enjoys worldwide reputation...")
- ❌ Separate contexts (Corporate vs Shop vs Experiences)
- ❌ Complex mega-menus with dozens of brand options
- ❌ Five different logo variations
