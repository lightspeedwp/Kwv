Inventory all existing content across the site:

### 2. Voice & Tone Transformation

Map current voice to required voice:

#### Current Voice (Handcrafted Wines)
- Identify tone examples from existing pages
- Categorize: formal, casual, technical, generic

#### Target Voice (The Wire Brand)
Required characteristics:
- Casual
- Friendly
- Passionate
- Welcoming
- Knowledgeable without formality
- Evocative, sensory, grounded in craft

#### Transformation Rules
Create specific rewriting guidelines:

**Avoid:**
- "Leveraging," "Solutions," "Premium offering"
- "State-of-the-art," "Utilize," "Facilitate"
- Generic luxury clichés
- Tourist brochure filler
- Overcomplicated tasting language

**Use:**
- "Hey," "You'll love," "We're pretty proud of"
- "Can't wait to," "Let's," "Honestly," "Absolutely"
- Warmth, confidence, charm
- Place-based storytelling
- Sensory descriptions
